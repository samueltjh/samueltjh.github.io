BOARD OF DIRECTORS


Freddy Chong

Founder & CEO

Freddy has been working in the payment industry the last 25 years with a keen focus on South Asia. Prior to being an entrepreneur, he was working with prominent U.S. MNCs like Hewlett Packard,Tandem Computers, United Parcel Service and Verifone. His last held position was as Managing Director of Wirecard (a German public listed company providing third party processing services to the payment industry) and was responsible for developing the Greater China and Indo-Chinaregion. Wirecard acquired his companies, Trans Infotech Pte Ltd and Card Techno Pte Ltd wholly in 2012.

Freddy brings with him a deep-rooted understanding and knowledge as well as the relevant business contacts that can facilitate and expedite growth into targeted markets. He graduated with a Master’s Degree in Business Administration from the Royal Melbourne Institute of Technology (RMIT).



Anthony Chong

Non-Executive Director

(Group Managing Director, Kingsmen Exhibits Pte Ltd)

Anthony drives the strategic management and day-to-day operations of the Group's Theme Parks, Museums, Exhibitions and Events businesses. He has more than 35 years of experience in marketing and the fulfilment of different disciplines that encompass world-class attractions, tradeshows, retail interiors and large-scale sporting & corporate events.

Anthony holds a Master’s Degree in Business Administration from Victoria University of Technology.



Carrie Chong

Executive Director

Carrie has a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Mass Communications from Royal Melbourne Institute of Technology (RMIT) and had a prominent career as a Producer-Presenter with Mediacorp Radio for 7 years, before making the switch to challenge herself in the payment industry.

Since then, she has amassed 9 years of relevant experience, primarily leading the sales and marketing aswell as merchant acquisition arm of a prominent terminal vendor, Card Techno Pte Ltd, which was subsequently acquired wholly by Wirecard Singapore in 2012.



William Mark Patrick

Non Executive Director

(CEO, GoSwiff Holdings Pte Ltd)

Mark has had 30 years of experience in the payment industry. He started his career with Verifone and joined MasterCard International before moving to Ingenico and landing at GoSwiff. Mark is a highlysuccessful and committed C-level Fintech Executive with expert knowledge of the electronic payments and e-commerce industries. He has also had valuable international experience building multi-million dollar businesses and capturing market share in Asia, Europe, the Middle East and Africa.

Mark holds a Master’s Degree in Business Administration from University of Technology, Sydney Australia.
